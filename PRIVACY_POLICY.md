## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password, payment method
- **Profile Data:** Company name (if applicable), subscription tier, account creation date
- **Billing Information:** Payment details processed through Stripe (we do not store full credit card numbers)

- **User-Generated Content:** TikTok creator handles, scan queries, notes, and bookmarks

### 3.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, clicks, time spent on platform
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Scan Results:** Brand deal detection results, AI analysis outputs, historical scan records
- **Cookies & Tracking:** Session cookies for account authentication and security

### 3.3 Third-Party Platform Data

To provide our Service, we access public data from:

- **TikTok:** Creator profiles, video captions, engagement metrics (via RapidAPI's tiktok-scraper7)
- **YouTube:** Channel data, video transcripts (via Transcript24 API)
- **Instagram:** Creator profiles, public posts (where accessible)

We do not store personal data of TikTok/YouTube/Instagram creators unless you explicitly save it in your RespawnSignal account.

## 9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **HTTPS/SSL Encryption:** All traffic is encrypted in transit
- **Password Hashing:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plaintext)
- **Secure Sessions:** Session tokens expire and require re-authentication
- **API Key Protection:** All secrets stored in server-side environment variables
- **Database Security:** Supabase provides encrypted database storage

However, no security system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
