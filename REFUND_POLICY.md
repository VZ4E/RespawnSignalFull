## 1. Overview

RespawnSignal is committed to customer satisfaction. This policy outlines how refunds and billing work for our service.

---

## 2. Refund Policy

### 2.1 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're unsatisfied with RespawnSignal for any reason, you're eligible for a **full refund within 7 days of your first charge**.

**How to Request:**
1. Email support@respawnmedia.co with your request
2. Include your account email and reason (optional)
3. We'll process the refund within 3-5 business days

**Refund Eligibility:**
- ✅ First-time subscribers within 7 days
- ✅ Full amount minus payment processor fees (~2-3%)
- ❌ Refunds are not available after 7 days
- ❌ Multiple refunds on the same account may be denied

### 2.2 Credit Packs (One-Time Purchases)

If you purchase additional credits or one-time add-ons:
- **Non-refundable** once purchased
- Credits do not expire while your account is active
- Upon account cancellation, unused credits are forfeited

### 2.3 Subscription Cancellation

When you cancel your subscription:
- You retain access to the Service **until the end of your current billing period**
- No refunds are issued for the current billing period
- Your data remains accessible until deletion (see Privacy Policy)
- Unused features or scans do not translate to refunds

**Example:**
- You're billed on the 1st of the month
- You request cancellation on the 15th
- You still have access through the 30th
- No refund for the remaining 15 days

### 2.4 Failed Payments & Disputes

If you dispute a charge via your bank or payment processor:
- Your account will be **immediately suspended**
- Chargebacks damage our ability to process payments
- We may terminate your account permanently
- You forfeit any remaining credits or subscription access

**Proper Dispute Resolution:**
If you have a billing issue, contact us first at support@respawnmedia.co rather than disputing with your bank.

## 3. Subscription & Billing Terms

### 3.1 Auto-Renewal

Your subscription renews automatically each month unless you cancel.

**Before Each Renewal:**
- We send a reminder email 7 days before renewal
- The email includes a cancellation link
- Clicking cancel stops future charges

### 3.2 Billing Cycles

| Plan | Price | Cycle | Renewal Date |
|------|-------|-------|--------------|
| **Pro** | $29/month | Monthly | Same date each month |
| **Max** | $79/month | Monthly | Same date each month |

**Example Timeline:**
- Sign up: March 26
- First charge: March 26
- Next renewal: April 26
- Reminder email: April 19

### 3.3 Payment Processing

We use **Stripe** to process all payments.

- All transactions are encrypted via HTTPS
- Your full credit card number is never stored by us
- Stripe handles PCI compliance
- Invoices are automatically generated and emailed

### 3.4 Failed Payment Handling

If your payment method declines:

1. **Day 1 (Failed):** We attempt to charge again
2. **Day 3:** Second retry attempt
3. **Day 5:** Final retry attempt
4. **After 3 failures:** Account suspension (access disabled)
5. **After 30 days:** Account terminated (data may be deleted)

**How to Restore Access:**
- Update your payment method in account settings
- We'll attempt to charge again immediately
- Service is restored upon successful payment

### 3.5 Refund Processing Timeline

All refunds are processed within:
- **3-5 business days** via your original payment method
- Payment processor fees (~2-3%) are deducted
- Stripe handles refund processing, not RespawnSignal

## 4. Price Changes

### 4.1 Advance Notice

We will notify you of any price increases via email **at least 30 days in advance**.

### 4.2 How Price Changes Work

**Scenario:** Pro plan increases from $29 to $35

- **Current Subscribers:** Locked in at $29 until you cancel and re-subscribe
- **New Subscribers:** Charged the new $35 price
- **Upon Renewal:** If you continue after the change date, you'll be charged the new price

### 4.3 Opting Out

If you don't accept a price increase:
- Cancel before the change takes effect
- No refund is issued for the current period
- You can re-subscribe at any time

## 5. Chargebacks & Fraud Prevention

### 5.1 What Is a Chargeback?

A chargeback occurs when you dispute a charge directly with your bank or credit card company instead of contacting us.

### 5.2 Consequences of Chargebacks

We take chargebacks seriously:
- Your account will be **immediately suspended**
- We will investigate and may terminate your account
- You forfeit all remaining credits and subscription
- You may be prevented from creating a new account
- Repeated chargebacks result in permanent account ban

### 5.3 Prevention

**Contact us first if:**
- You were charged twice
- You see an unfamiliar charge
- You forgot you had a subscription
- You believe there was a billing error

Email: support@respawnmedia.co

We will work with you to resolve any legitimate billing issues without involving payment processors.
