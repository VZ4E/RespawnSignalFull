## 4. Equity Structure

### 4.1 Vesting Schedule

Equity vests over **4 years** with a **1-year cliff**. This means:
- **Year 0-1 (Cliff):** 0% vesting (if a Founder leaves, they forfeit all equity)
- **Year 1-4:** Linear vesting of remaining equity
- Upon departure: Founder receives vested equity only

**Example:** If Founder has 50% equity:
- Leaves after 6 months: 0% (cliff not met)
- Leaves after 18 months: ~6.25% (50% × 1.5 years / 4 years)
- Leaves after 4 years: 50% (fully vested)

## 9. Non-Compete & Non-Solicitation

### 9.1 Non-Compete (During Tenure)

While a Founder is active in the Company, they agree **not to**:
- Work on competing products or companies
- Freelance on competing projects
- Accept employment with direct competitors

**Exception:** Side projects that don't compete with RespawnSignal are allowed with approval.

### 9.2 Non-Solicitation (After Departure)

For **2 years** after leaving the Company, a departing Founder agrees **not to**:
- Recruit current employees to a competing company
- Solicit customers for a competing product
- Hire any employee who worked on RespawnSignal

### 9.3 Scope

"Competitor" is defined as any service that:
- Detects brand deals on TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram
- Uses similar AI/ML analysis for creator intelligence
- Is sold to agencies or brands for creator research

## 10. Departure & Exit Terms

### 10.1 Voluntary Departure

If a Founder voluntarily leaves:
- **Vested equity** remains theirs
- **Unvested equity** is forfeited back to the Company
- They must sign a **Founder Separation Agreement**
- Their equity is subject to repurchase terms (see below)

**Example:** Founder with 50% equity leaves after 2 years
- 2 years of a 4-year vest = 25% vested
- 25% remains with Founder (can be repurchased)
- 25% reverts to Company

### 10.2 Equity Repurchase

Upon departure, the Company has the **right to repurchase** the Founder's vested equity at **fair market value** (or lower, as determined by board).

**Repurchase Timeline:** Must be exercised within **30 days** of departure.

### 10.3 Involuntary Departure (Fired)

If the Company terminates a Founder for cause (violation of this Agreement, fraud, misconduct):
- **All equity** (vested and unvested) is forfeited
- Founder receives **no compensation**

If termination is **without cause:**
- Vested equity remains (subject to repurchase)
- 6 months of unvested equity vests as severance

### 10.4 Death or Disability

If a Founder becomes incapacitated or dies:
- Vested equity passes to their estate/beneficiaries
- Unvested equity reverts to the Company
- The Company will attempt to repurchase vested equity from the estate

## 14. Dispute Resolution

### 14.1 Mediation (First Step)

If Founders disagree on the interpretation of this Agreement:
1. Attempt to resolve through discussion (7 days)
2. If unresolved, engage a neutral mediator
3. Each Founder pays their own legal costs; mediator costs split 50/50

### 14.2 Arbitration (If Mediation Fails)

If mediation fails, disputes will be resolved via **binding arbitration** under New York law:
- Single arbitrator chosen by mutual agreement
- Location: New York, USA
- Loser may pay arbitrator fees (judge's discretion)

### 14.3 No Litigation

Founders agree **not to sue each other** directly. All disputes must go through mediation and arbitration first.
